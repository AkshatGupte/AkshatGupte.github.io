01. About Me

I'm a student who likes building things with machine learning. Most of my recent projects have been around deep learning and LLMs — a CNN that grades diabetic retinopathy from retinal scans, a multi-agent system where agents work together to debug an outage, and a chatbot that lets you analyze a CSV file just by asking questions about it.

I've also spent some time on the research side, looking into whether the attention weights in temporal models actually explain what the model is doing. Outside of projects, I'm usually practicing problems on LeetCode.
